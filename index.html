Foodie :Smart Calorie Tracker for Modern Eaters

Foodie is a modern and intuitive calorie tracking app designed to help users take control of their diet, understand their nutritional intake, and achieve their health goals—whether it’s weight loss, muscle gain, or simply maintaining a balanced lifestyle. With its sleek interface and intelligent features, Foodie makes tracking what you eat simple, insightful, and even enjoyable.

Foodie stands out with its user-friendly design and a holistic approach to wellness. Whether you're a meticulous macro counter or just want to be more mindful about what you eat, Foodie adapts to your style.
